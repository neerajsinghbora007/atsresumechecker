Why Resumes Fail

Why Indian Job Seekers Don't Get Shortlisted in 2026

These are the 6 problems Neeraj Singh Bora fixes in every resume rewrite — problems that apply whether you are in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, or Hyderabad.

  • ✗
    Your resume format fails ATS parsing. Over 75% of Indian resumes are rejected before any human reads them. Canva, Zety, two-column, and design-heavy templates fail Workday, Taleo, and Naukri Resdex parsing completely. The content never gets read.
  • ✗
    Wrong keywords for your target role and industry. Generic summaries like 'seeking a challenging role' score near zero on every ATS system. Role-specific keywords — pulled from actual job descriptions at your target companies — are what get resumes ranked.
  • ✗
    Naukri profile not optimized alongside the resume. Naukri scores your uploaded resume and your profile separately. Most candidates update one. Resdex recruiters run bulk searches against both. Ignoring your profile means losing direct recruiter approaches.
  • ✗
    Expensive services with no India-specific knowledge. Most resume services charge ₹2,000–₹5,000 and are written by teams who do not know the difference between Workday, Taleo, and Resdex. India's hiring systems are specific — a generic ATS-friendly resume is not enough.
  • ✗
    Outdated resume sections that signal an old document. Declaration, Date of Birth, Father's Name, Photograph — these flag your resume as outdated to both ATS systems and human recruiters. They take up space that could be used for keywords and achievements.
  • ✗
    Duty-listing instead of measurable achievements. Every candidate at your level has held similar responsibilities. What differentiates you is results: percentages, numbers, time saved, revenue generated. ATS systems also score quantified bullets higher than generic duty lines.

ATS Systems

4 ATS Systems Indian Employers Use — And How I Optimize For All of Them

Most resume services say "ATS-friendly" without knowing which system your target company actually uses. Each one parses differently. I research the specific platform before writing every resume.

Workday

Used by HDFC, Tata, Unilever HUL, Reliance, Mahindra. Strict single-column requirement. Two-column or table-based resumes produce garbled text during parsing.

Taleo (Oracle)

Used by ICICI, Wipro, Infosys, Siemens India, Godrej. Date formats must match exactly. Keyword matching is literal — synonyms are not recognized.

Naukri Resdex

India's largest recruiter database. Profile and uploaded resume scored separately. Recruiters across all sectors run daily bulk searches here.

SAP SuccessFactors

Used by Reliance, Mahindra, L&T, Bajaj Auto. Strong keyword matching on the professional summary section. Generic summaries consistently score low.
