Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about reaching and working with ATS Resume Checker.

How do I contact ATS Resume Checker?

+

The fastest way is WhatsApp. Click any of the WhatsApp buttons on this page to open a pre-filled message. Neeraj Singh Bora personally responds to every message, typically within 1–2 hours during 9 AM – 9 PM IST, 7 days a week. You can also email nrjbora@gmail.com for formal queries.

What information should I share when I contact you?

+

Three things: (1) your current resume in any format — PDF, DOCX, or even a rough list; (2) the job role or industry you're targeting; (3) any specific companies or portals you're applying to. You don't need a polished resume to start — a rough draft works perfectly fine.

How quickly will I get a response?

+

WhatsApp messages are typically answered within 1–2 hours during business hours (9 AM – 9 PM IST). Emails within 24 hours. Resume delivery is within 24 hours of receiving all required details — including weekends.

Is there any free consultation available?

+

Yes. You can start by using the free ATS score checker — it runs 16 checks on your resume in 30 seconds with no login needed. You can also WhatsApp with quick questions and Neeraj will give you honest, no-obligation advice on whether your resume needs a full rewrite or just minor fixes.

Can I get help if I'm applying for Gulf or UAE jobs?

+

Yes — Gulf market resumes are a specialty. Just mention the target country and role when you WhatsApp. Pricing is the same starting at ₹499. Gulf resume requirements (format, length, content expectations) differ from Indian domestic requirements and Neeraj handles both.
