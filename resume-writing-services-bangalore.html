Why Bangalore Job Seekers Don't Get Shortlisted in 2026

These are the real reasons your resume is not getting calls — specific to Bangalore's hiring systems, not generic advice.

  • ✗
    Your resume format fails Greenhouse and Workday parsing. Bangalore's top tech companies — Google, Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy — use Greenhouse. IT services firms use Workday. Both systems reject multi-column and design-heavy resumes. Your content never gets read.
  • ✗
    Generic IT skills do not differentiate you in Bangalore's market. Every Software Engineer resume lists Java, Python, and Agile. Without role-specific project language, quantified impact, and the exact tech stack your target JD lists, your resume scores the same as hundreds of others.
  • ✗
    Startup resume framing does not work for MNC applications. Bangalore's market splits between product companies, IT services, and startups — and each expects different resume framing. Resume and CV writing: a startup resume that emphasizes ownership and cross-functional impact needs restructuring for an Infosys or TCS application.
  • ✗
    Naukri Resdex optimization is ignored by most IT professionals. IT professionals in Bangalore apply through company portals and LinkedIn, but ignore Naukri. Recruiters at IT services firms still run Resdex searches daily — a strong Naukri profile scores passive approaches you'd miss otherwise.
  • ✗
    Canva and visual resumes fail every parser Bangalore companies use. Greenhouse, Workday, and Naukri all reject table-based and column-heavy formats. A two-column resume from a Canva template has a near-zero chance of surviving Greenhouse's parsing at Google or Flipkart.
  • ✗
    No quantified achievements, only duty descriptions. Bangalore's competition level means 800–2,000 applications per role. Resumes with measurable bullets (reduced deployment time by 40%, improved API response by 25%) consistently rank higher than generic duty descriptions.

ATS Systems

Bangalore Employers Use These ATS Systems — Is Your Resume Ready?

Most resume writers say "ATS-friendly" without knowing which system your target company in Bangalore actually runs. Each parses differently. I research before writing every resume.

Greenhouse

Used by Google India, Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, and most Bangalore product startups. Scores on keyword density and resume section structure. Fails multi-column formats completely.

Workday

Used by Infosys, Wipro, Accenture Bangalore, and many IT services MNCs. Single-column only. Date format mismatch is the most common rejection reason.

Naukri Resdex

Still the primary sourcing tool for IT services recruiters at TCS, Infosys, HCL. Profile and uploaded resume scored separately. Daily recruiter searches happen here even for Bangalore's IT services market.

Lever

Used by Razorpay, CRED, and many funded Bangalore startups. Modern parser — scores on skills-based keywords and project language. Generic IT resumes do not stand out here.
