ATS Systems

Delhi NCR Employers Use These ATS Systems — Is Your Resume Ready?

Most resume writers say "ATS-friendly" without knowing which system your target company in Delhi NCR actually runs. Each parses differently. I research before writing every resume.

Workday

Used by HUL, P&G, Maruti Suzuki, Deloitte India, and most Gurgaon-based MNCs. Single-column only. Any table or sidebar causes text merge during parsing.

Taleo (Oracle)

Used by some FMCG companies and mid-size Gurgaon MNCs. Literal keyword matching — no synonym recognition. Resume dates must match the expected format exactly.

Naukri Resdex

Primary sourcing tool for Noida IT firms and Delhi-based services companies. Profile and resume scored separately. Recruiters run daily searches — an optimized profile generates approaches without you applying.

iCIMS

Used by consulting firms and professional services companies in Gurgaon. Skills-based filtering. Your skills section needs to explicitly list the exact terms used in the JD.
