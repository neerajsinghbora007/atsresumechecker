He has personally written, reviewed and optimized more than 10,000 resumes for professionals across India, the Gulf region, and beyond — from fresh graduates applying on Naukri for the first time, to Senior Managers and CFOs targeting leadership roles at MNCs.

What makes Neeraj different from a typical resume writer is his dual perspective: he's been on both sides of the table. His Philosophy

Neeraj believes that a bad resume isn't a reflection of a bad candidate — it's almost always a fixable formatting or keyword problem. Most job seekers in India are genuinely qualified for the roles they're applying to. They're just losing to robots before any human ever sees their name.

His writing style — both in resumes and in blog content — is deliberately jargon-free and practical. He doesn't believe in fluffy career advice. Everything he writes is based on what he's seen work in actual hiring processes at Indian companies and multinational corporations operating in India.
