ResumeWorded
Useful but US-focused
Cost: $19/month
Login required: Yes
India-specific: No

ResumeWorded is one of the better-known ATS checkers globally and gives reasonably detailed feedback on structure and keyword usage. The problem for Indian users is that its keyword database and scoring are built around US companies and US ATS systems. When I tested my Indian IT resume, it flagged several things as "missing" that are completely standard in Indian resumes, and missed issues that specifically affect Naukri and Indian ATS parsing. The paywall also means you can't really test it without committing to a subscription.

Jobscan
Good for US, limited for India
Cost: $49.95/month
Login required: Yes
India-specific: No
Jobscan is the most technically sophisticated keyword matching tool on this list — it takes your resume and a specific JD and gives you a detailed match report. For someone applying to US companies, it's genuinely useful. For Indian job seekers, the price ($49.95/month = roughly ₹4,150) is hard to justify, especially when the tool doesn't understand Naukri's algorithm, Indian ATS platforms, or India-specific hiring conventions. For that price, you could get your entire resume professionally rewritten twice.

  • Overkill for most Indian job seekers
