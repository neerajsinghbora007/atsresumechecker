Frequently Asked Questions — ATS Resume Templates India

What is an ATS-friendly resume template?

An ATS-friendly resume template uses simple formatting — single column, standard fonts, no tables or graphics — so Applicant Tracking Systems on Naukri and LinkedIn can correctly read and rank your resume. Fancy Canva templates often fail ATS because the software cannot parse design elements.

Which resume format is best for freshers in India?

Freshers should use a single-column ATS format: Education first, then Skills, then Projects/Internships, then any experience. Standard fonts like Arial or Calibri, saved as PDF. Avoid photos, tables, and decorative elements which lower ATS score on Naukri.

Can I use these templates for Naukri?

Yes. All 11 resume templates are designed for Naukri, LinkedIn Easy Apply, and Shine. They score 80+ on Naukri's Resdex ATS system. Neeraj Singh Bora has personally reviewed 500+ Naukri resumes and these templates use the exact format patterns that score highest.
