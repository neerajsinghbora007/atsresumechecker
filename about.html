India Focus

Why India-Specific Expertise Matters

ATS Resume Checker is specifically built for the Indian job market — not adapted from a global service. This distinction matters in several concrete ways:

Naukri.com Optimization

Naukri.com is India's largest job portal and has its own internal ranking algorithm that determines which candidates appear first in recruiter searches. Optimizing for Naukri requires understanding how its search indexes candidate profiles — which is different from how Workday or Taleo (US ATS systems) work. Global services like Jobscan or ResumeWorded are calibrated for US systems and do not account for Naukri's ranking factors.

Indian ATS Platforms

Many Indian companies use ATS systems that are not common in the US market — Zoho Recruit, iSmartRecruit, Darwinbox, Freshteam, and GreytHR. Each has its own parsing quirks. A resume that passes Workday may not parse correctly on iSmartRecruit. ATS Resume Checker's 16-point structural check is calibrated against these Indian platforms specifically.

Gulf and UAE Market

A significant portion of Indian professionals target employment in the Gulf — UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait and Bahrain. Resume formatting requirements for Gulf markets differ substantially from Indian domestic requirements. ATS Resume Checker offers dedicated Gulf market resume writing as part of its standard service.

Pricing in Indian Rupees

Global resume services typically charge $20–$25 per month — roughly ₹1,700–₹2,100 monthly, or ₹20,000+ annually. ATS Resume Checker charges a one-time fee of ₹499 per resume. For most Indian job seekers, this makes professional resume writing genuinely accessible rather than a luxury.
