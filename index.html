What is an ATS resume and why does it matter?

+

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) resume is formatted and keyword-optimized to pass automated screening software. Over 99% of Fortune 500 companies and most Indian companies including TCS, Wipro, Infosys, and Flipkart use ATS. Studies show that 75% of qualified candidates are rejected before a human ever sees their resume — simply because of poor formatting or missing keywords. An ATS-optimized resume fixes this.

Why is my resume being rejected even though I'm qualified?

+

The most common reasons are: using tables, columns, or graphics that ATS cannot read; missing keywords from the job description; non-standard section headings; saving as the wrong file format; or having a resume that's too long or too short. Our free ATS checker identifies exactly which of these is hurting you — and we fix all of them in our rewrite service.
